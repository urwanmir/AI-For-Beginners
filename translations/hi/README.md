## Aap kya seekhenge

**[Course ka Mindmap](http://soshnikov.com/courses/ai-for-beginners/mindmap.html)**

Is curriculum mein aap seekhenge:

* Artificial Intelligence ke alag-alag approaches, jisme "purana" symbolic approach jisme **Knowledge Representation** aur reasoning ([GOFAI](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Symbolic_artificial_intelligence)) shamil hai.
* **Neural Networks** aur **Deep Learning**, jo modern AI ki buniyad hain. Hum in important topics ke concepts ko do sabse popular frameworks - [TensorFlow](http://Tensorflow.org) aur [PyTorch](http://pytorch.org) - mein code ke zariye samjhayenge.
* Images aur text ke saath kaam karne ke liye **Neural Architectures**. Hum haal hi ke models cover karenge, lekin shayad bilkul state-of-the-art mein thodi kami ho.
* Kam popular AI approaches, jaise ki **Genetic Algorithms** aur **Multi-Agent Systems**.

Is curriculum mein hum kya cover nahi karenge:

> [Is course ke saare additional resources hamare Microsoft Learn collection mein dekhein](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/collections/7w28iy2xrqzdj0?WT.mc_id=academic-77998-bethanycheum)

* **AI in Business** ke business cases. Microsoft Learn par [Introduction to AI for business users](https://docs.microsoft.com/learn/paths/introduction-ai-for-business-users/?WT.mc_id=academic-77998-bethanycheum) learning path, ya [AI Business School](https://www.microsoft.com/ai/ai-business-school/?WT.mc_id=academic-77998-bethanycheum), jo [INSEAD](https://www.insead.edu/) ke saath milkar banaya gaya hai, dekhne par gaur karein.
* **Classic Machine Learning**, jo hamare [Machine Learning for Beginners Curriculum](http://github.com/Microsoft/ML-for-Beginners) mein achhe se bataya gaya hai.
* **[Cognitive Services](https://azure.microsoft.com/services/cognitive-services/?WT.mc_id=academic-77998-bethanycheum)** ka use karke banaye gaye practical AI applications. Iske liye, hum aapko Microsoft Learn ke [vision](https://docs.microsoft.com/learn/paths/create-computer-vision-solutions-azure-cognitive-services/?WT.mc_id=academic-77998-bethanycheum), [natural language processing](https://docs.microsoft.com/learn/paths/explore-natural-language-processing/?WT.mc_id=academic-77998-bethanycheum), **[Generative AI with Azure OpenAI Service](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/training/paths/develop-ai-solutions-azure-openai/?WT.mc_id=academic-77998-bethanycheum)** aur dusre modules se shuru karne ki salaah dete hain.
* Specific ML **Cloud Frameworks**, jaise [Azure Machine Learning](https://azure.microsoft.com/services/machine-learning/?WT.mc_id=academic-77998-bethanycheum), [Microsoft Fabric](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/training/paths/get-started-fabric/?WT.mc_id=academic-77998-bethanycheum), ya [Azure Databricks](https://docs.microsoft.com/learn/paths/data-engineer-azure-databricks?WT.mc_id=academic-77998-bethanycheum). [Build and operate machine learning solutions with Azure Machine Learning](https://docs.microsoft.com/learn/paths/build-ai-solutions-with-azure-ml-service/?WT.mc_id=academic-77998-bethanycheum) aur [Build and Operate Machine Learning Solutions with Azure Databricks](https://docs.microsoft.com/learn/paths/build-operate-machine-learning-solutions-azure-databricks/?WT.mc_id=academic-77998-bethanycheum) learning paths use karne par gaur karein.
* **Conversational AI** aur **Chat Bots**. Iske liye ek alag [Create conversational AI solutions](https://docs.microsoft.com/learn/paths/create-conversational-ai-solutions/?WT.mc_id=academic-77998-bethanycheum) learning path hai, aur aap zyada detail ke liye [is blog post](https://soshnikov.com/azure/hello-bot-conversational-ai-on-microsoft-platform/) ko bhi dekh sakte hain.
* Deep learning ke peeche ki **Deep Mathematics**. Iske liye hum Ian Goodfellow, Yoshua Bengio aur Aaron Courville ki likhi [Deep Learning](https://www.amazon.com/Deep-Learning-Adaptive-Computation-Machine/dp/0262035618) suggest karenge, jo online bhi available hai [https://www.deeplearningbook.org/](https://www.deeplearningbook.org/) par.

_Cloud mein AI_ topics ka halka intro pane ke liye, aap [Azure par Artificial Intelligence ke saath shuru karein](https://docs.microsoft.com/learn/paths/get-started-with-artificial-intelligence-on-azure/?WT.mc_id=academic-77998-bethanycheum) learning path le sakte hain.

## Har lesson mein hota hai

* Pre-reading material
* Executable Jupyter Notebooks, jo aksar framework (**PyTorch** ya **TensorFlow**) ke liye specific hoti hain. Executable notebook mein kafi theoretical material bhi hota hai, toh topic ko samajhne ke liye aapko kam se kam notebook ka ek version (chahe PyTorch ho ya TensorFlow) dekhna hoga.
* **Labs** jo kuch topics ke liye available hain, jo aapko seekhi hui cheezon ko kisi specific problem par apply karne ka mauka deti hain.
* Kuch sections mein [**MS Learn**](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/collections/7w28iy2xrqzdj0?WT.mc_id=academic-77998-bethanycheum) modules ke links hote hain jo related topics cover karte hain.

## Shuruat Kaise Karein

### 🎯 AI mein naye hain? Yahan se shuru karein!

Agar aap AI mein bilkul naye hain aur jaldi se haath saaf karna chahte hain, toh hamare [**Beginner-Friendly Examples**](./examples/README.md) dekhein! Inme shamil hain:

- 🌟 **Hello AI World** - Aapka pehla AI program (pattern recognition)
- 🧠 **Simple Neural Network** - Bilkul zero se neural network banayein
- 🖼️ **Image Classifier** - Images ko classify karein detailed comments ke saath
- 💬 **Text Sentiment** - Positive/negative text ko analyze karein

Ye examples isliye banaye gaye hain taaki aap full curriculum mein ghusne se pehle AI ke concepts samajh sakein.
